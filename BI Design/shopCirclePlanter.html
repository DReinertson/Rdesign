Product Description

Designer wall-mounted planters are the perfect blend of wall-art and greenery. Powder coated steel make these a practical option for indoor as well as outdoor settings. Magnetic pot allows for a perfectly centered plant/floral arangement - or off center, if that's your thing! Express your unique style by hanging as a single planter or in tandem with other Modern Air Head shapes, sizes and colors. For inspiration, visit our gallery.

Hanging Instructions

Use a single nail with an ample, flat nail head. Hammer nail into wall so the nail head is as close to the wall as possible, leaving only the minimum allowance necessary to accomodate the the thickness of the metal. This method ensures a tight fit flush to the wall and a perfectly parallel planter. Approx. 1" projection from wall, has a "floating" appearance.
