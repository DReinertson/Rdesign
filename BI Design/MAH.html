The Modern Air Head collection of versatile wallscape planters offers a variety of simple, artistic solutions for showcasing air plants, succulents and cacti and they are the perfect solution for the well-designed vertical garden!

Hang solo or in multiples to create your own unique gallery wall and show off your creativity by hanging Modern Air Head wallscape planters with our wallscape tea light tiles for a truly inspired environment.

Made of powder coated, galvanized stainless steel in combination with magnetic planters of ceramic, wood and glass – the wall tiles can be hung indoors or outside and look beautiful in residential and commercial settings. Plants not included.
