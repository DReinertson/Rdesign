Modern Airhead

The Modern Airhead collection of wall-mounted planters are the perfect blend of wall-art and greenery in a variety shapes, sizes and colors! Grouped MoAH vertical planters create artistic installations and the powder coated, steel construction is practical for indoor or outdoor use. Showcase your tillandsia, succulents, cacti and other plants. Oh, and faux greenery looks just as fab! Magnetic pot allows for a perfectly centered arrangement every time.

Collaborations & Custom

MoAH collaborates with artists and craftspeople to create limited edition collections. We can also create custom, large scale commercial and residential vertical planters. To inquire, please Email BI Design.
