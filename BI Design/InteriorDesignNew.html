BI Design is a boutique interior design company specializing in high-end residential renovations and ground-up projects on Bainbridge Island, WA and its surrounding areas, including Seattle. Designer, Denise Stoughton maintains a collaborative space in which clients can participate in the design process as much or as little as is comfortable. The motto she lives by is magical transformation, believing beautiful spaces positively impact the lives of those who dwell within.
