Helpful Hanging Tips We've Learned Along the Way


Measure your wall space (width and height). Lay out planters in your desired pattern on the floor. Mark all nailholes on the wall with a pencil before you put your first nail in. Remember to take into account the size of the planters as well as the size of the spaces in between: size of planters + size of spaces = total design area.

Alternating circles and squares are easier to hang than trying to hang squares only. You can hang all squares but your measurements have to be spot on!

Three to four inch spacing between 12" planters works nicely. Two to three inch spacing between 9" planters.
