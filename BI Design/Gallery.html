What's so cool about MoAH is that the possibilities for creating your unique wallscape are pretty much, endless! The Gallery shows a few ideas to get you going.

Helpful hanging hints we've learned along the way:

Measure your wall space (width and height). Lay out planters in your desired pattern on the floor. Mark all nailholes on the wall with a pencil before you put your fist nail in. Remember to take into account the size of the planters as well as the size of the spaces in between: size of planters + size of spaces = total design area

Alternating circles and squares are easier to hang than trying to hang squares only. You can hang all squares but your measurements have to be spot on!

Three - four inch spacing between 12" planters works nicely. Two - three inch spacing between 9" planters.
